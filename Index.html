Understanding Climate Change

Climate change refers to significant changes in global temperatures and weather patterns over time. It is caused by human activities such as burning fossil fuels and deforestation.

Climate Impact

Climate change affects ecosystems, weather patterns, and sea levels. It poses significant threats to biodiversity and human health.

Solutions

There are many ways we can combat climate change, including renewable energy, conservation efforts, and sustainable practices.
